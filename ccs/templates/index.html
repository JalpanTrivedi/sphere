About Us


‘Center for Computer Studies’, an initiative of Prof. (Dr.) Pallvi Mangal, BCA, MCA, Ph.D aims at uplifting academic aspirants. The key fundamental of the Institute is the adoption of current trends and understanding the future need of the Industry. It looks forward to the overall development of the student, not only by bridging the gap between the student and Technology but also imparting personality development sessions so as to deliver a product who can be an asset to the society.

Vision


The Center aims to develop IT professionals who are fully equipped with the necessary technical skills to meet the ever-growing challenges of the Industry. For this, we believe in imparting high quality, industry relevant and cost effective training.

Our long term vision is to bring about a revolution in the field of Information Technology (IT) by providing expert and advanced knowledge of today and tomorrow’s IT industry requirements.
